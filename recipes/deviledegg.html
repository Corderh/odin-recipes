Deviled Eggs Recipe

Description

Lets be "dead ass", deviled eggs are fucking sick. After you eat these deviled eggs you, yourself, are going to be sick.

Prep time: 15 mins

Servings: 6

Yield: 12 delicious motherfuckin' deviled eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs, Duh.
  • Mayonnaise
  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Mustard
  • Onion and Celery
  • Salt and Paprika

Directions

  1. Cut the boiled eggs in half and remove the yolks. Reserve the whites.

  2. Mash the yolks with a fork and add the remaining ingredients.

  3. Stuff or pipe the filling into the egg white halves.
